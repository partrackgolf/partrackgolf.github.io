Privacy Policy

ParTrack · Effective May 24, 2026

ParTrack is built to keep your data private. When you play by yourself, nothing leaves your device. When you choose to play with friends in a private tournament, only the data needed to run that tournament is shared, and only with the people you invite.

This policy explains what data the app handles, where it lives, and the choices you have.

Solo play — what stays on your device

  • Course information and round history are stored locally on your device.
  • iPhone ↔ Apple Watch sync uses Apple's WatchConnectivity framework to mirror your courses, favorites, and rounds between your own devices. This sync is mediated by Apple and never passes through our servers.
  • Scan Scorecard uses your iPhone's camera to capture an image of a paper scorecard. The image is processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework for text recognition. The image is not uploaded anywhere and is discarded after parsing.

Tournament play — what we collect and share

When you create or join a private tournament with friends, the following data is collected and shared with the other participants in that tournament:

  • Display name — the name you choose to show other players.
  • An identifier tied to your iCloud account — used so the app can recognise you across your devices and within tournaments you join. We do not see your Apple ID, email address, or real name.
  • Round data for that tournament — the course played, your strokes per hole, and timestamps. This is what powers the live leaderboard.
  • Tournament membership — which tournaments you are part of and which other players are in them.

This data is collected only when you actively use tournament features. If you never use them, nothing is collected or shared.

Where your data lives

  • On your device (and paired Apple Watch) for all solo data.
  • In your private iCloud and in a shared iCloud zone for each tournament, using Apple's CloudKit service, for tournament data. We do not operate any servers of our own. Apple stores this data on your behalf, governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Who can see your data

  • Your solo data: only you, on your devices.
  • Your tournament data: only the people you invited or accepted into that tournament.
  • Us (ParTrack): we have no servers and cannot see your data.
  • Third parties: we never sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers, analytics providers, or any other third party.

What we don't do

  • We don't run any servers or backend of our own.
  • We don't include analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We don't track you across other apps or websites.
  • We don't ask you to create an account with us — tournament identity uses your existing iCloud account.

Permissions

  • Camera: requested only when you tap "Scan with Camera" in the Scan Scorecard flow. Used to capture an image of a paper scorecard for on-device text recognition.
  • Network access: used only when you create or join a tournament, to sync data with other participants through Apple's CloudKit service.
  • iCloud: required for tournament features. If you are not signed in to iCloud, you can still use solo and Watch-sync features normally.

Your choices

  • Delete all local data: uninstall the app from your device.
  • Leave a tournament: removes your data from that tournament's shared zone.
  • Delete your tournament data: available from within the app's settings.
  • Email us at partrack.golf@outlook.com if you want help deleting any data we may hold.

How long we keep tournament data

Tournament data persists for as long as the tournament exists. When a tournament is deleted by its host, or when you leave it, your data for that tournament is removed from the shared zone. Local copies of your own rounds remain on your device until you delete them.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from anyone in that age group.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy, or requests about your data? Email partrack.golf@outlook.com.
